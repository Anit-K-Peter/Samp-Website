Server Rules And Regulation
- MetaGaming (MG)

While on our server, it is strictly prohibited to watch streams of any player currently active in-game or on any platform. Stream sniping will result in severe consequences, including potential permanent bans. Using out-of-character information gathered from streams, stream comments, Discord chats, etc., is also considered MetaGaming.

- Fear RP (FRP)

Players must display fear and comply with armed threats or weapons pointed at their characters. Ignoring these situations or failing to roleplay fear will result in penalties.

- PowerGaming (PG)

Abusing in-game factions or jobs for personal gain over other players is not allowed. This includes coercing other players into actions for personal benefit.

- Random Death Match

Randomly attacking or harming another player without a valid roleplay reason is prohibited.

- Vehicle Death Match

Deliberately using a vehicle to harm another player is strictly prohibited. Unintentional incidents will be reviewed, but deliberate or repeated offenses will result in warnings and bans.

- Death Match (DM)

Attacking another player without a proper in-character motive or interaction is not allowed. Players must have a valid reason supported by prior roleplay interactions.

- Combat-Log

Disconnecting or logging out during a roleplay scenario to avoid consequences is considered Combat-Logging. Players must remain connected and participate in active roleplay situations.

- Non-Roleplay (Non-RP)

Behaviors that are unrealistic or detract from quality roleplay are not allowed. Players are encouraged to seek staff guidance for clarification on roleplay expectations.

- Lore-Breaking

Intentionally disregarding established lore or events affecting characters or scenarios is prohibited.

- Revenge Roleplay

Seeking revenge based on out-of-character reasons rather than in-character motivations is not allowed.

- Baiting

Provoking situations solely to initiate combat or force roleplay conflicts is against the rules.

- Not Valuing Life (NVL)

Behaviors that disregard character life or promote low-quality roleplay are not tolerated.

- Give & Take Roleplay

Cooperation between players to enhance roleplay scenarios is encouraged. Players should prioritize scenario integrity over personal outcomes.

- New Life Rule (NLR)

Upon death or incapacitation, players must adhere to the New Life Rule, which includes forgetting recent events and refraining from rejoining ongoing scenarios.

- Verbal Abuse

Using offensive language or behavior towards other players is not tolerated.

- Flow Of RP

Adapting to the flow of roleplay scenarios rather than challenging them is encouraged for better gameplay experiences.

- Torture RP

Actions that repeatedly target a player or extend roleplay discomfort beyond reasonable limits are not allowed.

- Cop Baiting

Provoking law enforcement without valid in-character reasons is considered non-roleplay.

- Common Decency

Players are expected to treat each other with respect and create a welcoming environment within the community.

- Toxicity and Bullying

Toxic behavior, including imposing unwelcome attitudes on others, is strictly prohibited.

- Real Money Trading (RMT)

Exchanging in-game items, currency, or accounts for real-world currency is not allowed.

- Bug Exploiting

Intentionally exploiting game bugs or glitches for personal gain is strictly forbidden.

- Car Jacking

Players cannot steal vehicles if the owner is nearby and objects. Admin intervention may occur if reported.

- Ninja Jacking

Forcefully removing a player from a moving vehicle to steal it is prohibited and will result in consequences.
